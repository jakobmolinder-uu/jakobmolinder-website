Data

Datasets on Swedish historical labour-market statistics that I have compiled and made publicly available. When using them, please cite the associated publication where one exists, or the dataset itself (Jakob Molinder, dataset title, version 1.0). The strike data is formally citable via the Swedish National Data Service. Feel free to get in touch with questions.

Swedish National Unemployment Rate According to Available Sources, 1911–2010

The national unemployment rate in Sweden from five different sources, covering most years between 1911 and 2010. Version 1.0.

Zip (Excel + Stata) Excel (.xlsx) Stata (.dta)

Swedish Public Labour Exchange Data by County, 1914–1975

Number of applications, applicants, vacancies, and filled vacancies at the public labour exchanges, by county, for some or all years 1914–1975. Includes original data and standardized county tables. Version 1.0.

Zip (Excel + Stata) Excel (.xlsx) Stata (.dta)

Swedish Public Labour Exchange Data by Industry, 1916–1946

Number of applications, applicants, vacancies, and filled vacancies at the public labour exchanges, by industry, for some or all years 1916–1946. Includes original data and standardized industry tables. Version 1.0.

Zip (Excel + Stata) Excel (.xlsx) Stata (.dta)

Geocoded Dataset on Strikes in Sweden, 1859–1938

Geocoded records of strikes and labour conflicts, compiled through the project “From Sundsvall to Saltsjöbaden: A Regional Approach to Strikes and Protests in the Swedish Labor Market, 1859–1938.” Archived and citable at the Swedish National Data Service.
